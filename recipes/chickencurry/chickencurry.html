Chicken Curry Recipe

This butter chicken dish is a lovely rich curry - without the spicy heat of some curries. It comes with plenty of sauce to soak into the rice!.

Rich and creamy butter chicken recipe, a little bit of up front prep is all you need, then you can cook this dish in less than 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Ginger
  • Paprika
  • Onion
  • Salt
  • Curry Powder
  • Sugar
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Marinade chicken and leave for 1 hour (up to overnight)
  2. Blend onion, garlic and ginger together
  3. Heat butter and oil in large pan over medium-high heat
  4. Add the blended mix from step 2 and cook for 5-6 minutes
  5. Add marinated chicken and cook for 3-4 minutes until sealed
  6. Add salt curry powder and other spices stir for 1-2 minutes then simmer for 15 minutes
  7. Stir in cream and allow heat through
  8. Serve over rice
