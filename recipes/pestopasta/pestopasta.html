Pesto Pasta Recipe

Ingredients

  • 12 oz dry spaghetti
  • Salt
  • 3 Cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup parmesan
  • 1/4 cup pine nutes
  • 2 medium garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin oil
  • 1/2 cup pasta water

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water
  2. Make pesto sauce by blending basil leaves, parmesan, pine nutes and garlic
  3. Toss pasta and pesto after draining spaghetti
